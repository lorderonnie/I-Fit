5 unhealthy breakfast foods


1: Breakfast Cereals,reason being cereals are highly processed and contain only a small amount of whole grains. Also, nutrients are artificially added in a process called fortification.

2:Pancakes and Waffles,pancakes and waffles are typically topped with pancake syrup, which contains high-fructose corn syrup.

3:Toast With Margarine,Toast with margarine raises your blood sugar and insulin levels, causes rebound hunger and increases your risk of weight gain and heart disease.

4:Muffins,Muffins are usually high in refined flour, refined vegetable oils and sugar, all of which are very unhealthy.

5: Fruit Juice(Processed juice):Some fruit juices on the market actually contain very little juice and are sweetened with sugar or high-fructose corn syrup.Which is bad for the body.
